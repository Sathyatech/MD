MD Automations is a high-energy environment driven by talented people passionate about their work. We partner with international operations in Australia, North America, Asia, and Europe to tackle complex business issues and navigate new markets.

We Innovate Everything

MD Automations and Controls is the trusted leader in providing Embedded,VLSI solutions to industries and Engineering Educational Institutes. Our People, Our Culture and the way we work with our clients makes us to survive in this work. Since the Company's inception in 2002, MD Automations has fostered an environment that promotes striving higher in everything we do. We provide an cost effective solutions for all custom based problems.

We seek to change the world through innovation. Our team is open to new ideas with the potential to expand market share, improve profitability, or streamline oper- ations.
